Mathematical Framework

1. Frontier AI Progress (Exogenous Driver)

$$g(t) = \frac{g_0}{1 + \exp[-\sigma(t - t_0)]}$$

Logistic growth fitted to Epoch/Stanford cost-per-token series (2022-25)

2. Knowledge Dynamics

$$\dot{K}_g(t) = \beta_g(t) \cdot d_g(t) \cdot s_g(K_g(t)) \cdot g(t) \cdot K_g(t)^{\phi}$$

Where:

  • Œ≤_g(t): Learning rate multiplier (endogenously adapts)
  • d_g(t): Present bias factor (quasi-hyperbolic discounting)
  • s_g(K): Cognitive saturation function = 1/(1 + œà_g K)
  • œÜ: Complementarity exponent (knowledge returns to scale)

3. Learning Rate Adaptation

$$\dot{\beta}_g = \eta_g[\lambda_g G_g - \varrho_g L_g]$$

Loss/gain adaptation based on recent learning experiences

4. Higher-Order Dynamics

Velocity: $V_g = \dot{K}_g$

Acceleration: $A_g = \beta_g d_g s_g g K_g^{\phi-1}[\phi V_g + K_g(\dot{g}/g - \psi_g V_g)]$

Jerk: $J_g = \dot{A}_g$ (subject to human limit J^max ‚âà 0.4)

5. Gap Evolution

$$\Delta(t) = \ln[K_H(t) / K_L(t)]$$ $$\dot{\Delta} = g(t)[\beta_H d_H s_H K_H^{\phi-1} - \beta_L d_L s_L K_L^{\phi-1}]$$
